FAQ
Frequently asked questions
PPF is a transparent paint protection film applied to painted exterior surfaces to help protect against scratches, stone chips, insects, stains, and daily driving damage.
Light surface scratches can recover through the film's self-healing coating. Recovery performance may vary depending on scratch depth, film condition, and surrounding temperature.
Warranty coverage differs by product. Namsan Apex is listed as a 5-year warranty product, while Namsan Desert is listed as 5–7 years. Final warranty terms should follow the official warranty policy in each market.
Installation cost depends on vehicle size, coverage area, installer pricing, and market conditions. Use the coverage options above as a guide, then contact a local installer or dealer for a quotation.
Yes, ceramic coating may be applied over PPF when the coating is compatible and applied correctly. Always follow the coating manufacturer's instructions and allow the film to settle after installation.
Avoid aggressive washing, high-pressure water near film edges, harsh chemicals, and picking at edges during the initial settling period. Professional installers should provide final aftercare instructions.
Film life depends on product grade, installation quality, climate, washing habits, and vehicle usage. Proper care helps maintain appearance and protection performance over time.
